FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How is gravel laid?

A: Laying gravel is an easy way to make spaces look better.

Ground is assessed, and we determine whether leveling is necessary
Setup begins, where we help you choose the right landscaping fabric for your application
We fix the landscaping fabric, and determine quantity and type of gravel needed
We lay the gravel and level it, ensuring it looks as perfect as you envisioned it.

About Packages & pricing

A: We have a few basic packages for one-time services, such as mowing, gravel, or home repair and can customize to your liking. We like to take on projects, and offer the best pricing with bundled services. Pricing varies by dimensions, service type and material costs associated. Please refer to our Packages section, and contact us if you have any questions.

What home services do you offer?

A: We typically do anything from installing fixtures, applying vinyl, to addressing minor leaks or issues around your home. Since clients have different needs and need varying solutions, it's best to let us know in detail, and have us take a look for the best pricing and results. We offer, but are not limited to, painting, sanding, drywall, wood flooring, moving and minor repair.

Can you purchase items on my behalf for my project?

A: Yes, we prefer to. We know every item that is used for the task, and often do the shopping for our clients, then provide receipts. It is preferred to have us do the shopping so that we can accommodate for the necessary parts, as well as use our expertise to ensure what's essential is purchased.
