FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What types of handyman services do you offer?

A: We provide a wide range of home repair, installation, and maintenance services — everything from fixing drywall and leaky faucets to mounting TVs, installing shelves, repairing fences, and replacing light fixtures. Whether it’s indoor or outdoor work, small jobs or multi-day projects, we help homeowners keep their properties in top shape without the hassle of calling multiple contractors.

Do you handle both small repairs and larger projects?

A: Yes, we do! No job is too small. Whether it’s tightening a door hinge, or repairing drywall we can help. For larger projects, we coordinate the work efficiently and provide transparent quotes, so you know exactly what to expect from start to finish.

Do you serve residential, commercial, or both?

A: We serve both! We work with homeowners, landlords, and property managers for residential jobs, as well as small businesses that need maintenance or installations in offices and retail spaces.

Can you assemble furniture or exercise equipment?

A: Definitely! We assemble furniture, gym machines, outdoor playsets, office desks, and more. We handle the tools, instructions, and setup so you can skip the frustration and enjoy the finished product.

Do you handle minor plumbing or electrical installations?

A: Yes, we can handle simple replacements such as faucets, showerheads, outlets, switches, and light fixtures. For anything that requires new wiring or plumbing lines, we’ll connect you with a licensed specialist to keep things up to code.

How do I book a handyman appointment?

A: You can call, text, or fill out our online booking form. We’ll discuss your project details, provide an estimate, and schedule a time that works for you. For bigger jobs, we may visit in person first to give a more accurate quote.
