FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How is gravel laid?

Laying gravel is an easy, low-maintenance way to make spaces look better. Here's how we do it:

The ground is assessed and we determine whether leveling is necessary. Setup begins, where we help you choose the right landscaping fabric for your application. We fix the landscaping fabric and determine the quantity and type of gravel needed. Finally, we lay and level the gravel, ensuring it looks as perfect as you envisioned.

Safety and quality are our top priorities. If the weather prevents us from working safely or could compromise the results, we'll pause the project and reschedule as soon as conditions improve. We'll keep you informed every step of the way and make sure your project is completed with the highest level of care.

What are the benefits of adding mulch to my garden beds?

Mulch does much more than just make your garden look tidy — it's a powerful tool for plant health. A good mulch layer helps retain soil moisture (especially valuable in Lethbridge's hot, dry summers), suppresses weeds, and regulates soil temperature to protect roots from both heat and sudden chinook thaws. Over time, organic mulches like bark or wood chips also break down and improve soil quality. Plus, mulch gives your garden beds a polished, finished appearance with less ongoing maintenance.
