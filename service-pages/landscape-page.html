How is gravel laid?

A: Laying gravel is an easy, low maintenance way to make spaces look better. Steps:

Ground is assessed, and we determine whether leveling is necessary
Setup begins, where we help you choose the right landscaping fabric for your application
We fix the landscaping fabric, and determine quantity and type of gravel needed
We lay the gravel and level it, ensuring it looks as perfect as you envisioned it.

What are the benefits of adding mulch to my garden beds?

A: Mulch does much more than just make your garden look tidy — it’s a powerful tool for plant health. A good mulch layer helps retain soil moisture (especially valuable in Lethbridge’s hot, dry summers), suppresses weeds, and regulates soil temperature to protect roots from both heat and sudden chinook thaws. Over time, organic mulches like bark or wood chips also break down and improve soil quality. Plus, mulch gives your garden beds a polished, finished appearance with less ongoing maintenance.
