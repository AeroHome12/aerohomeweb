FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does drywall mudding and finishing take?

A: Drywall finishing usually takes 2–4 days, depending on the number of coats, drying time, and humidity. Each layer of mud must dry completely before sanding and applying the next coat to ensure a smooth, durable finish.

Is drywall dust dangerous?

A: Drywall dust isn’t toxic, but it can irritate your eyes and lungs if not handled properly. We minimize dust by using proper containment, vacuums, and clean-up systems so your home stays safe and tidy throughout the project.

What painting services do you offer?

A: We provide interior and exterior painting for homes, garages, fences, and commercial spaces. Our services include surface prep, priming, painting, touch-ups, and finish coatings using top-quality paints for long-lasting results.

Can you fix wall imperfections before painting?

A: Yes — every paint job includes basic wall prep such as filling small holes, sanding rough spots, and priming repaired areas. If you have larger cracks or damage, we can do drywall repair before painting to ensure the surface is perfect.

Can you repair damaged drywall instead of replacing it?

A: Yes — in most cases, we can repair damaged drywall rather than replacing entire sections. Whether it’s holes from wall anchors, cracks from settling, or moisture damage, we cut out the affected area, patch it cleanly, and blend it with the surrounding surface. For water damage, we always check that the source of the issue (like a leak or humidity problem) is fixed before we repair the wall. Once finished, the repair area is sanded smooth and painted to match, leaving no visible trace of damage.

Do you help with color selection?

A: Yes — choosing the right color can be overwhelming, so we’re happy to help. We can suggest shades that complement your lighting, flooring, and décor, or even provide sample swatches so you can see how colors look in different times of day. Lethbridge homes often benefit from warm neutrals or earthy tones that soften natural light and handle the dry climate well. If you’re refreshing multiple rooms, we can also create a cohesive color palette that flows beautifully throughout your home.
